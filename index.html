Our Mission Statement

Healthy Father's Healthy Children Corp shall operate exclusively to provide leadership, training, psychotherapy, communication skills, conflict resolution, and self worth through education and mentorship to restore and build relationships between Fathers, Children, and their families. Part of our goal is to extend our services to seniors in our communities and to evaluate individual needs and provide critical community services.
